Gunning Fog Test

The Gunning Fog readability formula calculates the U.S. grade level of a document based on its number of sentences and complex words (i.e., words that contain three or more syllables).

Note that numerals and proper nouns are always treated as easy words for this test, regardless of their syllable count.

The Gunning Fog Formula

G = .4*(W/S+((H/W)*100))

Where:

G U.S. grade level
W Number of words
H Number of 3+ syllable words
S Number of sentences

Citations

Gunning, R. (1952). The Technique of Clear Writing.
