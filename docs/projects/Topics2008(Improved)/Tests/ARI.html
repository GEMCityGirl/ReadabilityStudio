ARI Test

The Automated Readability Index– also called “ARI” or “auto”–was originally created for U.S. Air Force materials. As such, it was designed for technical reports and manuals. This test calculates the U.S. grade level of a document based on sentence length and character count.

The ARI Formula

G = (4.71 * (C/W)) + (0.5 * (W/S)) - 21.43

Where:

G U.S. grade level
W Number of words
C Number of characters
S Number of sentences

Citations

Smith, E. A. and R. J. Senter (1967). 'Automated readability index', AMRL-TR, 66-22. Wright-Patterson AFB, OH: Aerospace Medical Division.
