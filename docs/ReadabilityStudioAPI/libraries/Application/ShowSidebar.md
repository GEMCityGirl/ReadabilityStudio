## ShowSidebar {-}

Show or hides the project's sidebar\index{User interface!project sidebar}.

```{sql}
ShowSidebar(boolean show)
```

### Parameters {-}

**Parameter** | **Description**
| :-- | :-- |
show | **true** to show the sidebar, **false** to hide it.
