## SetRightPrintHeader {-}

Sets the label to appear in the top right corner of printouts.

### Syntax {-}

```{sql}
SetRightPrintHeader(Label)
```

### Parameters {-}

**Parameter** | **Type** | **Description**
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
Label | **string** | The label to appear in the top right corner of printouts.
:::
